Vision: Beyond Retail Reaction Cycles

Why 90% Traders Lose

Most discretionary behavior compounds cognitive bias: overtrading after wins, revenge after losses, and narrative anchoring during uncertainty. Without statistical framing, confidence replaces evidence.

Emotional vs Probability Trading

Emotion-centric systems treat every setup as binary conviction. Probability-centric systems measure edge quality, distribution of outcomes, and decision confidence relative to regime context.

Risk-of-Ruin Priority

Expected value is insufficient without sizing discipline. Risk-of-ruin must govern leverage, stop logic, and capital allocation to ensure sustainability through inevitable drawdown cycles.

Volatility Regime Filtering

A valid strategy can underperform in the wrong volatility phase. Regime classification prevents deploying trend logic in compression or mean-reversion logic in expansion.
