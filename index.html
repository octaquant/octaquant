The Problem

Why 90% of Traders Lose

Most participants operate without tested position sizing rules, expectancy tracking, or drawdown controls. Performance variability is treated as noise rather than a measurable risk system variable.

Emotional vs Probabilistic Trading

Emotional trading overweights recent outcomes and narrative conviction. Probabilistic trading evaluates distributions, confidence intervals, and regime suitability before allocating risk.

Risk-of-Ruin

A strategy can remain statistically positive yet still fail if leverage and streak exposure are unmanaged. Risk-of-ruin modeling is fundamental to long-horizon survival.
